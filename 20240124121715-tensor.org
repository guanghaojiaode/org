#+title: Tensor

- Def.: an algebraic object that describes a multilinear relationship between sets of algebraic objects related to a vector space.
- independent of any basis
- zero order: scalar
- first order: vector
- second order: matrix
- third order: 3-d space
  
- ref: [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tensor]]
