#+title: Radiation Types
There are three types of radiation.
+ [[id:92689def-4574-4c31-b212-efe665175706][Beta Ray]] $\beta$
  * negative electrons
+ [[id:d5d30aae-8b2d-4c66-86ff-f412ea70e90b][Gamma Ray]] $\gamma$
  * neutral electromagnetic radiation
+ [[id:6327f42b-fd13-4f01-a27c-e08d3732c395][Alpha Ray]] $\alpha$
  * positive He-4 2+
  * very similar to the X-ray but with different frequency. Gamma ray has higher frequency.
