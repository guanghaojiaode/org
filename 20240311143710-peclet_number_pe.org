#+title: Peclet Number (Pe)
- ${\displaystyle \mathrm {Pe} ={\dfrac {\mbox{advective transport rate}}{\mbox{diffusive transport rate}}}}$
- Pe = 0: advection negligible, diffusion dominates the mass transport
- Pe = 1: advection & diffusion the same
- Pe = infinite: diffusion negligible, advection dominates.
